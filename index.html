About CloseoutHQ

CloseoutHQ was built for the people who actually run the job site — project managers, construction managers, and their crews — not for a back office.

Why it exists

Punch lists live on clipboards, in text threads, and in people's heads. Items slip, vendors show up to the wrong work, and nobody's sure what's actually done. It keeps every open item tied to a room, a trade, and a vendor — and gets it to the right person in a format they'll actually open.

Who it's for

Homebuilders managing multiple homes through every phase, from foundation to closing — and the trades who finish them.

What makes it different

Built phone-first for the field. Vendors don't need the app — they get a clean PDF or Excel work order by text or email. And it speaks your crews' languages, not just English.

How it works

Five steps, start to finish.
1
Add your homes
Create each address, set its commitment date, and place it in a build phase.
2
Lock a trade & log items
Walking a house for paint? Lock "Paint," then rattle off items by room — they all auto-assign. Unlock to switch trades.
3
Send the work order
Share a PDF or Excel with photos straight from your phone — by text or email. No app needed on their end.
4
Track completion
Vendors mark work done and leave a note if something's held up. You get notified.
5
Watch the dashboard
See every home's phase, open items, milestones, and what needs attention — at a glance.
